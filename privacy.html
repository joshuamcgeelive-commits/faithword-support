Privacy Policy for FaithWord Last Updated: November 5, 2025 Introduction FaithWord ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application FaithWord (the "App"). Information We Do Not Collect FaithWord is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically: We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information We do not collect location data We do not access your camera or photo library We do not collect device identifiers beyond what is required for advertising We do not require account creation or login We do not track your browsing activity outside the app Information Stored Locally All game progress, including: Puzzles solved Coins earned Daily login streaks Achievement badges unlocked Settings and preferences ...is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers. Third-Party Services Google AdMob FaithWord displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes, including: Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) IP address Device type and operating system App usage data related to ad performance For more information about how Google uses data, please see: Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy Google AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 Ad Personalization: On iOS, you will be asked for tracking permission. You can choose to deny this permission, and you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization Ad Removal Option: You can remove all advertisements by purchasing our one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase for $2.99. Apple App Store When you download FaithWord from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect information according to their privacy policy: Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ Children's Privacy FaithWord is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data, the app is safe for children to use. Parents and guardians should be aware that the app may display advertisements unless the "Remove Ads" purchase is made. Data Security Since all game data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted, your information is as secure as your device. We recommend: Keeping your device updated with the latest security patches Using device passcode/biometric protection Being cautious about who has physical access to your device In-App Purchases FaithWord offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements ($2.99). This purchase is processed through the Apple App Store and is subject to Apple's terms and privacy policy. We do not store or process any payment information. Your Rights Since we do not collect or store your personal information on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete, modify, or provide to you. All your game data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by: Uninstalling the app Clearing the app's data in your device settings Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by: Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy Posting a notice in the app (for significant changes) Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy. Data Retention All game data (progress, coins, streaks, badges) is stored locally on your device indefinitely until you: Uninstall the app Clear app data Factory reset your device We do not retain any data on servers. International Users FaithWord can be used worldwide. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no cross-border data transfers. The only data transmission occurs through Google AdMob for advertising purposes (see "Third-Party Services" above).
